About Us

Mirage Reality Labs is an indie game studio focused on building experiences that stay with players long after the screen fades. Our work blends stylized art, immersive atmosphere, experimental mechanics, and thoughtful game design.

Great games thrive on strong worlds, emotional impact, and meaningful player discovery. Every title we build on WebGL, PC, mobile, and VR is guided by this belief.

As a small, passionate team, we prototype fast, iterate boldly, and push ourselves to create deeply engaging experiences.
