About Us

Mirage Reality Labs is an indie game studio focused on building interactive experiences across games, tools, and creative technology. Our work spans Unity games, WebGL applications, and experimental AI-driven systems. We blend stylized visuals, strong mechanics, and performance-focused development. Beyond games, we build practical creative tools and share knowledge through tutorials and technical videos, contributing to the wider game development community.
